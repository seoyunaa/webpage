Academic Homepage

Yuna Seo

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of History at Kyungpook National University. I received my Ph.D. in History from Peking University (北京大学). My doctoral dissertation, Study of the Princesses of the Mongol-Yuan Dynasty (元朝公主群体研究——以《元史•诸公主表》所载为中心), examines the collective history of Yuan princesses. My research focuses on the Mongol Empire and the Yuan dynasty, especially political history, royal women, elite families, and regional governance.
