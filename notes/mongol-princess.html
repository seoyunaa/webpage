Overview

Project Scope

The Mongol Princess project focuses on royal women, marriage alliances, and related kinship structures in the Mongol Empire and Yuan China. This page will document how public-facing project materials are being organized.

What Will Be Collected Here

Notes on prosopographical structure, research categories, website-facing revisions, and future outputs will be gathered here as the project takes shape.
